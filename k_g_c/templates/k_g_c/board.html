KGC Board Members

The church is governed by the Board members who are elected by members every two years. The Board is in charge of overseeing the proper management of the church finances, church accommodation and the general welfare of the church following the laid down guidelines and procedures as per KGC Constitution. The current board members include:

  Pastor Eunice Ndirangu

  Chair Jane Njambi

  Sec Christine Kweri

  Tress Paul Kahara

  Finance Sec Hannah Wambui

  Member Lucy Thairu

  Member Paul Kang'ari

  Member John Kibatia
